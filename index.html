Breathwork, the practice of controlling breath to enhance physical, mental, and spiritual well-being, has roots in ancient traditions. It dates back thousands of years, with origins in yoga (India) and pranayama (breath control techniques). In China, Taoist practices incorporated breathwork for energy flow and longevity. Similarly, Buddhist meditation emphasized mindful breathing for focus and clarity.
